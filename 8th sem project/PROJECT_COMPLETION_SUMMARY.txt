ðŸ” HYBRID DETECTION SYSTEM

**5 Rule-Based Heuristics:**
1. RATE_SPIKE: request_rate > 15 req/s
2. ERROR_BURST: error_rate > 50%
3. BOT_PATTERN: low entropy + repeated params
4. LARGE_PAYLOAD: avg_payload > 5000 bytes
5. ENDPOINT_SCAN: unique_endpoints > 20

**Hybrid Scoring:**
